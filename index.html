Want to invite all your followers?

This is a simple tiny Javascript Bookmarklet to Invite All Your followers To a Studio for the new studio update!

How To Use

  1. Drag and drop the Link below to your bookmarks/favorites bar. If you don't see the bar, press Ctrl+Shift+ B (Windows) or Cmd+Shift+ B (Mac).

    Bookmarklet

  2. Go to the studio that you would like to invite your followers to.

  3. Drag it into the studio page.

  4. Follow the instructions.
